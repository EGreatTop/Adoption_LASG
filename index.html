About us

The Ministry of Youth and Social Development of the Lagos State Government (LASG) is saddled with the responsibility of handling adoption processes in a timely, well documented process meeting international standards.

Adoption

This is a global process controlled by government all over the world. Eligible applicants who meet the specified criteria across geographical locations can finally take care of their beloved adopted child(ren). You can start now.

Babies Home

These are specialized homes with professional personnel with the passion for children. They care for motherless babies ensuring they are well taken care of. These homes may be private or governent owned meeting the required regulations.

Adoption Process

This process begins with an application you can carry out online from the comfort of your home. This is followed up by direct calls from an assigned personnel for your application. you can monitor your status online from any of your devices.

About LASG

The Lagos State Government governs Lagos State in Nigeria. With multiple ministries covering various areas of administration in the state, the Ministry of Youth and Social Development oversees all processes involved eith adoption in Lagos State. Local Residents and International applications are welcome.
